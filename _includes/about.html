About

Welcome to the House of yay!

Home of soulful, funky, disco house grooves.

djrobyay is a rising star in the house music scene, hailing from Las Vegas with a passion for creating funky, soulful, and disco-infused mixes.

rob was introduced to house music in 1996 at Junior Vasquez's legendary Tunnel events in New York City. The infectious energy of the scene left a lasting impression, and he continued to explore the underground desert party scene in Southern California.

Drawing inspiration from the likes of Mark Farina, Joe D'Espinosa, and Dylan Drazen, rob has been honing his new craft in private and for friends. Now, he's ready to take his talents to the next level, seeking opportunities to spin publicly in LGBT-friendly spaces. His sets are characterized by their high-energy, feel-good vibes.

With a deep love for the genre and a dedication to his craft, this new comer is poised to make a big impact on the house music scene. Keep an eye out for djrobyay as he brings his unique sound to a club or festival near you!
